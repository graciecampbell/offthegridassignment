What is Off the Grid Living?
  • Off the grid living is a lifestyle that revolves around sustainability and reducing resources used. Most of the time, off of the grid living means to live in less populated areas and away from busy cities.

    • Best Places to Live

  • 1. Montana-Plenty of land available to purchase and start a new lifestyle 2. Oregon-Beautiful land and lots of resources available 3. Washington-Lots of open space and sustainability is important in Washington!
    • What is sustainability?

    Sustainablity is seen as a way for humans and the planet to exist in harmony! It can be described as "meeting the needs of the present generation without compromising the ability of future generations to meet their needs"
